The Azarverse: chapters 1-10

Chapter 1 - The Beginning

It happened a long time ago. Azarus was a youtuber that used to upload Incredibox content. One day, a man in a suit came to his house to tell him something no one would have expected. "You have been elected as a test subject for a top secret experiment.", said the man. Azarus, with no idea of what the man was saying, politely told him to leave his house. But the man insisted, pointing at him with a gun. "This is an offer you cannot refuse. Please come with us.", said this man. Last thing Azarus saw was that gun before going blank. Someone knocked him out.

He later woke up in an empty room, with nothing but a wooden chair. Suddenly, a voice spoke: "Thank you for your cooperation in the Breach Riding Experiment of Antiparticles and Decontamination. The project consists in you going through a spatial breach that suddenly opened in this planet. We guarantee your safety as long as you follow our instructions." Then Azarus replied: "Uhhhh... what was this thing called?" The voice spoke again, saying "In your pocket you will find a gadget with a circular shape. We want you to enter the spacial rift for one minute. You may enter the rift when the door opens. When you find yourself in the other side, take it out and let it do the work. If it is impossible for you to stay that long due to unforseen circumstances, you are allowed to return from where you entered. Leave the gadget in there if you cannot retrieve it." Azarus simply nodded in agreement.

After a few seconds, a door opened in the wall, revealing a bright light. It was the rift the voice was talking about. Azarus hesitated for a moment, but he had no other choice so he gathered some courage, closed his eyes and entered the rift. After he entered, an alarm went off. The lights turned bright red. The rift begun to suck everything that was nearby. "The rift is destabilizing!" "All systems are overheating!" "We need to close the rift immediately!" As the last voice spoke, the rift collapsed. All that was left was an empty room, with no traces of Azarus. No one could locate him. Not even with the built-in geolocation system nor the subspacial detection was able to pinpoint his location. Why was he nowhere to be found? He couldn't have possibly traveled to another universe, had he?
