Archive α - The Origin of a Diety

After the war of Embers, a hole opened up in the ground. It led to the abyss. But what no one knows is that the catastrophe also created something worse. It agitated the spacetime tissue so much that it opened a crack. The first rift ever recorded in the history of the multiverse. But it didn't open in that universe. It instead opened up in the Void universe. Why? Quite simple really. It's the most vulnerable universe since reality broke apart. The thing is, there lived one of the many versions of Azarus. He was the worst of the worst. He seeked entertainment through the suffering of others. Definitely a man to be feared, and very powerful. He grew tired of his own universe so the moment he saw the spatial rift he knew it was the best opportunity to leave this world behind and expand his realm.

Archive γ - Rifts

They are small cracks in the fabric of reality that connect universes. We don't know what caused them to appear, but it's possible that some sort of event caused them to appear in every universe. As of the time updating this archive, the investigation has reached the conclusion that these rifts only allow the many Azaruses to go through them. It has also concluded that something is making them. We still don't know who or what though.

Archive μ - Lazarus's early life

Lazarus was a normal person ever since he was conscious. One day just like any other day the war of embers broke out. As the government was recruiting soldiers, Lazarus had no choice but to enlist in the army. Thanks to having played many FPS games, he was granted a position as a sniper. At least he was safe from the chaos in the frontlines. During one mission in which he had to guard some sort of complex from anyone that tried to spy from afar, a large hole opened up in the ground, leading to what people called the depths. He was sent there to investigate and on many missions thereafter. Not much is known about what he's done in there, but when he returned he looked like he'd seen a lot of stuff. And somehow he had a cloak? Something was off with him. It was like he changed a lot in a short period of time.

Archive ν - Alternate universes

Archive ο - Lazarus

One of the core founders of the Association, he leads all the efforts to maintain the balance of the multiverse.

It's unknown what type of entity he is (or became), as he holds certain superhuman powers. He's native of the "Sep" universe, which at the time was an absolute mess. Even though he tried to keep himself out of the big fights during the war, he was eventually forced into it to survive. Not long after, he found out of the happenings described in Archive α. Feeling guilty, he left the war and spent months secluded developing a tool to travel universes. He eventually joined forces with the other 3 core members, creating what's known as the Azarus Association.
