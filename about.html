About this page

This is a collection of all current knowledge about the Azarverse. I, Chronozarus, am responsible for writing and maintaining these archives so that all members of the Association have access to all available information.

If you think something is wrong or outdated, please report it with the updated information so that I can update everything as soon as possible. Thank you for your cooperation.

Please also note that this archive may not hold the most accurate information, as it lacks images and other information about the lesser known Subjects.
